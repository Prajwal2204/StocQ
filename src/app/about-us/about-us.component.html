ABOUT US

Financial markets are highly complex stochastic systems, Thus it is much more beneficial for us to predict prices on a short term basis rather than predict what is going to happen a few hours, days or even months down the line. Thus we will be using Deep Q learning as it focuses on the benefit of the Q value rather than modeling the market. as it can also quickly adapt to recent market conditions thus making it less prone to market structure changes.


We have found abundant data on financial platforms such as yahoo finance and other dedicated data providers. We can also extract data from trading terminals provided they allow us to write scripts on their platform, thus capturing very recent data and saving it to a file.

Our Main Objectives

  • Build a sustainable and fast model.
  • Implement a risk management strategy for the model.
  • Build an analytics tool to help people judge the results through a long term view.
  • Compare and test results to real life scenarios.
  • Connect to the real-time market for paper trade execution, if the model has turned out as we expected
